Welcome to PAJI

The Punjabi Association of Jacksonville Indians is a non-profit community organization dedicated to preserving and sharing the rich cultural traditions of Punjab. We host festivals, workshops, and charitable initiatives that unite our community throughout the year.

Whether you were born in Punjab, have roots in the region, or simply love the culture — you are always welcome here. Our doors are open to all who wish to celebrate, learn, and grow together.
