4th Annual Punjabi Mela

Join us for the 4th Annual Punjabi Mela — a grand celebration of Punjabi culture, community, and togetherness. Enjoy live Bhangra and Giddha performances, authentic Punjabi cuisine, cultural exhibitions, and activities for the entire family. This is our biggest event of the year, bringing Jacksonville's Punjabi community together for an unforgettable evening.

April 13, 2026 Community Center, Jacksonville 5:00 PM – 10:00 PM
